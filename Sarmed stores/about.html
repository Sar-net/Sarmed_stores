How We Started

Our journey began with a simple goal — to make healthcare more accessible and convenient for everyone in our community. What started as a small local madicine store has grown into a trusted name, known for quality service, genuine medications, and a passion for helping people feel better. We saw the need for a medicine store that not only sells medicine but also cares. That’s why we created Sarmed Stores — to combine professional healthcare with modern convenience like online ordering, free delivery, and friendly support.

Every step we’ve taken is driven by our belief that health should never be out of reach..

We Are Trusted Company

At Sarmed Stores, trust is the foundation of everything we do. From the very beginning, we’ve built our reputation on honesty, reliability, and genuine care for our customers. We understand that when it comes to health, there’s no room for doubt — that’s why every product we offer is 100% original, sourced from certified suppliers, and handled with the highest standards of professionalism.

Free Shipping

Enjoy free shipping on all orders - fast, reliable, and right to your doorstep!

Free Returns

We care about your satisfaction, and that’s why we offer a free return policy within 24 hours of delivery. If you receive the wrong item, a damaged product, or something that doesn’t meet your expectations, you can return it at no extra cost. Just reach out to us within 24 hours, and we’ll handle the rest—quick, easy, and completely free.

Customer Support

Our customer support team is always here to help. Whether you have a question about a product, need help placing an order, or want to follow up on a delivery, we’re just a message or call away. We provide fast, friendly, and reliable support 24/7 to ensure you always feel cared for. Your health is our priority, and we’re committed to giving you the best service every step of the way.

The Team

Muddassir Musa Jega

CEO/Co-Founder

Muddassir Musa Jega is the Substantive Bursar of Federal University Gusau, appointed in December 2022 for a five-year term. With over 20 years of experience in financial administration, he previously served as Deputy Bursar. He holds an ND, HND, B.Sc., two postgraduate diplomas, and a Master’s degree in Accounting and Finance. He began his career at the National Mathematical Centre, Abuja. Muddassir Muddasir is also the Chairman of SSANU FUGUS Branch and is known for his integrity, leadership, and service.

Muzzammil Muddassir

Co-Founder

Muzzammil Muddassir is a Geology graduate from Federal University Gusau (FUGUS). He began his early education in Abuja, attending Federal Government College and Ambassador Global Academy, then continued at Caliphate International School (junior) and Lala International School (senior). During his studies, he actively participated in programs such as borehole surveying and other geology-related fieldwork. Muzzammil is passionate about earth sciences, with a strong interest in field research and environmental sustainability.
